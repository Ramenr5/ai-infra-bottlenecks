## Strategic / product

- **BiCS FLASH** — Kioxia's 3D NAND architecture
- **8th-gen and 10th-gen BiCS** in development
- **8th-gen QLC** in production
- **KIOXIA EG7 Series SSDs** — first client product on BiCS gen 8 QLC; TLC-equivalent performance + better TCO
- **Western Digital co-development partnership** — shared R&D + manufacturing scale (legacy from Toshiba/WD JV era)

## Strategic position

- One of the 5 major NAND suppliers globally
- Roughly tied with WD-legacy (now [[SanDisk]]) on volumes
- **Pure-play NAND** like SanDisk but Japanese-listed
- Less HBM/DRAM exposure than Micron / SK Hynix / Samsung
- **Plans US ADR listing** would broaden investor access

## Why it matters

1. **Completes NAND supplier landscape** — Samsung + SK Hynix + Micron + SanDisk + Kioxia + YMTC. All five non-Chinese suppliers now in the wiki.
2. **Q1 FY26 → Q3 FY26 EPS inflection (¥34 → ¥162)** = independent primary confirmation of NAND shortage timing
3. **WD partnership** = shared fab capacity; Kioxia + SanDisk together = significant NAND share
4. **Japan-listed pure-play NAND** = alternative geography for NAND exposure vs SanDisk US listing
