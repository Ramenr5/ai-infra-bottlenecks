## Memory segment (the HBM mirror)

- **Memory revenue: 39% of systems revenue** (up materially)
- **DRAM: 27% of systems revenue (RECORD)**
- Lam = global leader in memory etch + deposition
- HBM ramp = direct driver of segment growth
- Customers: SK Hynix M15X, Samsung HBM lines, Micron HBM3E/HBM4

## Strategic context

- **Etch and deposition intensity changing dramatically** with new memory cycle (1C node transition)
- Transition expands Lam's served available market by **>20%**
- AI's power/efficiency requirements driving advanced-generation device transitions
- Less direct foundry exposure than AMAT (memory-tilted)
