## Data center / Neoverse story

- **CSS (Compute Subsystem) program** seeing adoption with AWS, Google, NVIDIA, Microsoft
- Royalty growth across smartphones, Edge AI, Physical AI, Cloud AI

## Why it matters

1. **Royalty-based exposure to every chip.** ARM captures rents on the host CPU in essentially every AI system — NVIDIA Grace, Microsoft Cobalt, AWS Graviton, Ampere, plus the embedded CPUs in countless ASICs.
2. **Data center royalty doubling YoY** = direct confirmation of hyperscaler ASIC + Arm-CPU adoption pace
3. **CSS adoption with all 4 major hyperscalers** = strategic moat — Arm is now selling pre-validated subsystems, not just core IP, which compresses the time-to-tapeout for custom ASIC programs
4. **Distinct from EDA tool model** ([[Synopsys]]/[[Cadence Design Systems]]) — royalty per unit shipped rather than tool subscription. Different sensitivity to volume vs design-house count.
