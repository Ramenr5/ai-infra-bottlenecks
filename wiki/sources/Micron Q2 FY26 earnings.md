## Why it matters

1. **Validates [[HBM allocation]] severity from a third source.** SK Hynix sold-out → Samsung qualifying with NVIDIA → Micron extraordinary growth = all three suppliers in lockstep. HBM scarcity is structural, not idiosyncratic.
2. **HBM4 NVIDIA Vera Rubin alignment** = Micron is sole-sourced or majority-sourced for some Rubin-class SKUs (taking share from SK Hynix at the margin)
3. **75% GM Q2 → 81% guide Q3** = HBM is the highest-margin segment in semiconductors right now
4. **US-headquartered diversification angle** — only US HBM supplier, natural beneficiary of CHIPS Act + supply-chain diversification rhetoric
5. **HBM4E in development for 2027** = supply window past current ramp; 24 HBM stack per chip per [[TSMC Q1 2026 earnings|TSMC 2029 roadmap]] needs HBM4E
