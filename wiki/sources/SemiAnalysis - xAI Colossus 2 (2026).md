**Publisher:** [[SemiAnalysis]]

> [!success] Primary source — Colossus 2 = first GW-scale datacenter
> xAI's Colossus 2 targets 1.1 GW (with potential 1.5 GW expansion). ~3.7× larger than Colossus 1 (300MW). Bypasses grid entirely via 1.9 GW of Doosan + VoltaGrid turbines + Tesla Megapacks.

## Regulatory innovation

xAI split power generation across state lines — Memphis (Tennessee) datacenter + Mississippi (Southaven) turbine facility — to navigate air permitting separately in each state. Novel regulatory arbitrage.
