## Custom silicon — MTIA

- **MTIA Gen 2 chips in production** across recommendation-model inference
- Designed with [[Broadcom]] (per Broadcom's AI customer disclosure)
- Reduces dependence on NVIDIA for the inference workloads MTIA serves

## AMD partnership (major)

Per [[AMD Q1 2026 earnings]]: **Meta committed up to 6 GW AMD Instinct GPUs with first 1 GW on custom MI450** — largest publicly disclosed hyperscaler commitment to non-NVIDIA silicon.

## Why it matters

1. **2× 2025 capex levels** — Meta's annual capex went from $80-90B framing to $125-145B in 18 months
2. **AMD 6 GW deal** = real diversification away from NVIDIA-only; first hyperscaler at this scale committing to AMD Instinct
3. **MTIA Gen 2 production** = custom silicon validating Broadcom partnership economics
4. **33% YoY revenue growth** at $56B scale = still healthy ad business funding the capex
5. Meta is the third largest hyperscaler capex committer (after MSFT $190B, AMZN ~$200B, on par with GOOGL)
