## Why it matters for the bottleneck thesis

1. **Verifies "HBM sold out through 2026"** — central claim in [[HBM allocation]] now anchored to primary
2. **47% operating margin Q3 2025 / 72% reported for early-2026** = extraordinary rent capture; HBM scarcity is real and is being monetized
3. **HBM4 mass production timing (Sept 2025)** matters for Vera Rubin / B200 successor accelerator generations — the supply window for advanced HBM is tightening, not loosening
4. **NVIDIA HBM4 ~70% allocation to SK Hynix** quantifies the supplier concentration. If Hynix stumbles on HBM4 ramp, NVIDIA Rubin shipments slow.
5. **M15X expansion** is the concrete capacity-addition asset; worth tracking on a future [[SK Hynix]] update
