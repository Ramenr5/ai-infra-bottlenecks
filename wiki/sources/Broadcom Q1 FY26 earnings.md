## Custom AI ASICs (XPUs)

Broadcom designs custom AI accelerator chips for:
- **Google (TPU)** — multi-year, multi-generation
- **Meta (MTIA)** — multi-billion-dollar relationship
- **OpenAI** — recently disclosed
- **Anthropic** — recently disclosed

Broadcom is the design partner / packaging coordinator; TSMC manufactures the actual silicon.

## Networking ASIC dominance

- **Tomahawk 6** switch chip entered volume March 2026 — **industry's first 102.4 Tbps Ethernet switch ASIC**
- **Jericho 4** fabric chip (51.2 Tbps) shipping since Aug 2025
- Designed to interconnect **>1 million XPUs across data centers**
- Effectively monopoly in Ethernet-based data center fabric switches

## Why it matters

1. **$100B+ AI revenue line of sight by 2027** — the largest forward AI commitment after NVIDIA. Anchors the [[Capex trajectory thesis]] from the supply side.
2. **Custom ASIC partnership model is durable** — Google, Meta, OpenAI, Anthropic all designing custom silicon via Broadcom. The hyperscaler ASIC pipeline is real and growing.
3. **Tomahawk + Jericho near-monopoly in Ethernet networking ASICs** = picks-and-shovels on every AI data center switch. Direct exposure to scale-out networking.
4. **VMware integration** is separate from but accretive to AI thesis — provides infrastructure software cross-sell into AI deployments
