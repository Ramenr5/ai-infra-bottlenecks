## Headline

[[Meta]] signed **20-year PPAs for 2,609 MW of carbon-free nuclear** from [[Vistra]]'s PJM plants (Jan 2026) — part of Meta's up-to-**6.6 GW** nuclear push (also TerraPower, Oklo). The template for merchant-nuclear monetization of AI demand.

## Why it matters

- **Merchant nuclear = pure margin in tight capacity markets** ([[PJM]] +9.3× capacity spike). Near-zero marginal cost; the entire capacity payment is margin.
- Meta's 6.6 GW nuclear push (Vistra + Oklo + TerraPower) confirms hyperscaler appetite for firm clean baseload
- Same playbook as [[Talen Energy]]'s 960 MW Amazon–[[Susquehanna nuclear plant]] deal and [[Constellation Energy]]'s Crane/TMI–Microsoft restart
