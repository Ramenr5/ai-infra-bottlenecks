## Why it matters

1. **Quanta is the EPC layer** between utility planning and data center power readiness. When utilities + hyperscalers + IPPs commit to new generation, Quanta installs the transmission, substations, and distribution to actually deliver power.
2. **$40.1B Electric backlog** = multi-year forward visibility on power infrastructure work
3. **Doubling transformer manufacturing capacity** is a direct response to the transformer-lead-time bottleneck flagged in [[Power & energy]]; Quanta now becoming a transformer manufacturer, not just an installer
4. **Picks-and-shovels exposure to every hyperscaler data center power build-out** — agnostic to which hyperscaler wins
5. Different from utility plays (NextEra, Vistra, etc.) — Quanta sells labor + materials per project, not power
