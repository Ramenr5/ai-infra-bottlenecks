## Custom AI ASIC business

- Custom chip revenue grew **from near zero in FY26 to $1.5B annual run rate**
- Expected to grow **>20% YoY in FY27** (faster than previously guided)
- Customer: Microsoft Maia is the publicly known design; others rumored

## Optical / interconnect business

- Management guided **interconnect/optical at 50%+ YoY growth in FY27** (vs 20%+ for custom ASIC)
- Drivers: **800G and 1.6T optical DSPs** (the chips inside transceivers)
- CXL switch attach rates
- Photonic Fabric ramps eventually

## Why it matters

1. **Hyperscaler ASIC duopoly** — Broadcom and Marvell are the two major partners for hyperscalers building custom AI silicon. Broadcom = Google TPU + Meta MTIA; Marvell = Microsoft Maia + others.
2. **Optical DSPs are inside every transceiver** — Marvell's chips power the 800G/1.6T transceivers from [[Coherent]], [[Lumentum]], and others. Doubly-exposed to scale-out networking buildout.
3. **50% optical growth guide** > **20% custom ASIC growth guide** = interesting signal that connectivity is the more constrained segment
