# Power Integrations — 1250V & 1700V PowiGaN for 800VDC AI Datacenters

**Publisher:** Power Integrations IR (press release)
**Date:** October 13, 2025
**URL:** [investors.power.com/news/news-details/2025/Power-Integrations-Details-1250-V-and-1700-V-PowiGaN-Technology-for-Next-Generation-800-VDC-AI-Data-Centers](https://investors.power.com/news/news-details/2025/Power-Integrations-Details-1250-V-and-1700-V-PowiGaN-Technology-for-Next-Generation-800-VDC-AI-Data-Centers/default.aspx)
**Also at:** [businesswire.com/news/home/20251013503136/en](https://www.businesswire.com/news/home/20251013503136/en/Power-Integrations-Details-1250-V-and-1700-V-PowiGaN-Technology-for-Next-Generation-800-VDC-AI-Data-Centers)

## Headline framing

POWI publicly disclosed its **higher-voltage PowiGaN portfolio (1250V HEMTs + 1700V integrated switch)** and an explicit collaboration with **NVIDIA** on 800VDC AI datacenter power architectures. First public framing of POWI as a participant in the 800VDC silicon race.

## Key facts (verbatim where possible)

### Product highlights
- **1250V PowiGaN HEMTs** — "industry-first"; "field-proven reliability"; >98% efficiency targeting in 800VDC rectifier and DC-DC stages
- **1700V PowiGaN switch** — integrated into InnoMux2-EP IC; supports 1000VDC input; >90.3% 12V-system efficiency in SR ZVS operation
- Cumulative deployed: **175 million GaN switches** in use (as of Oct 2025)

### NVIDIA collaboration (explicit quote)

> "Power Integrations is collaborating with NVIDIA to accelerate the transition to 800 VDC power."

### POWI executive quote

> "With rising AI power demands, moving to an 800 VDC input simplifies rack design, makes more efficient use of space and reduces copper usage."
> — Roland Saint-Pierre, VP Product Development

### What's NOT in the release

- No specific hyperscaler customer named beyond NVIDIA
- No specific design win disclosed
- No volume / production / sampling timeline
- No mention of Diablo 400 spec, Kyber rack, on-blade module, or specific rack power levels
- "Megawatt-scale racks" referenced but no specific kW figure

## Why it matters

- **First public disclosure of POWI's NVIDIA collaboration** on 800VDC — paired with Q1 2026 earnings call commentary 7 months later, this was the original signal
- 1250V and 1700V is the voltage-class window for **on-blade 800V-to-50V DC-DC** in the Phase 2 Kyber rack architecture (per [[SemiAnalysis - Inside the 800VDC Revolution (2026)]])
- "Industry-first 1250V GaN HEMTs" claim positions POWI's vertical-integrated PowiGaN platform as a leading-edge integrated-switcher option vs Infineon / Navitas / Innoscience discrete HEMT competitors
