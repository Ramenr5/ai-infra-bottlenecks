## Foundry Business

- Q1 earnings declined on off-peak seasonality
- **Q2: full utilization of advanced-node lines targeted**
- Q2 driver: HBM4 base-die supply (Samsung supplies its own HBM4 base wafers via internal Foundry)
- **SF2 yields: ~20% late 2025 → 55-60% early 2026** (per TrendForce)
- **SF2X (HPC) 2026; SF2A (auto) + SF2Z (with BSPDN) 2027**
- AI customer wins: **NVIDIA Groq 3 LPU**, Preferred Networks (PFN), Ambarella, **Tesla $16.5B long-term**
- TSMC vs Samsung Foundry share: TSMC ~44% in 2026

## Capex — record-breaking

- **FY2026 capex revised UP to ~110 trillion won (~$78B) — RECORD**
- Primary allocation:
  - **P4 Pyeongtaek (Korea)** — memory capacity expansion (HBM-focused)
  - **Taylor wafer fab (US, Texas)** — foundry leading-edge
- This is **larger than TSMC's $52-56B FY26 capex AND ASML's full-year revenue**

## 2027 supply outlook (the Samsung warning)
