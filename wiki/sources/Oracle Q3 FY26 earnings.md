## OpenAI $300B agreement

- **$300B contract value** with [[OpenAI]] for data center capacity
- **4.5 GW of planned facilities**
- Part of $500B [[Stargate]] initiative (Oracle + SoftBank + NVIDIA + OpenAI)
- Primary driver of Oracle's RPO surge

## Strategic positioning

- Oracle pivoting to **"GPU landlord"** business model for AI infrastructure
- 30,000 layoffs alongside $50B+ AI capex = capital reallocation toward infra
- Different from MSFT/GOOGL/AMZN — Oracle is fundamentally a database company building hyperscale cloud almost entirely on OpenAI demand
- High concentration risk: OpenAI relationship is existential

## Why it matters

1. **$553B RPO** is **larger than Microsoft Q3 revenue and Amazon Q1 revenue combined** — extraordinary forward visibility but extremely concentrated on OpenAI
2. **OpenAI $300B deal across 4.5 GW** = single largest AI infra commercial contract; supports [[Stargate]] $500B framework
3. **FY26 capex $50B (up from $21B)** = Oracle joining hyperscaler-tier capex commitment
4. **$45-50B debt + equity raise** = concrete evidence of [[AI debt financing capacity]] absorbing real volumes
5. **GPU landlord model** = new business archetype distinct from traditional cloud (more like project finance)
