## Why it matters

1. **Connectivity is a real bottleneck** between accelerator scaling and rack-scale deployment — when [[NVIDIA]]'s GB200 NVL72 wires together 72 GPUs at 900 GB/s NVLink, the signal integrity over copper at those speeds is non-trivial. Retimers/smart cables (Aries) are essential. ALAB is the merchant supplier.
2. **+93% YoY revenue + 76% GM** = extraordinary economics; ALAB is one of the cleanest "AI buildout" picks-and-shovels names in public markets
3. **Microsoft Azure M-Series Leo win** + **custom KV Cache for 2027** = leading-indicator of CXL memory expansion (relevant for [[Data wall|long-context]] inference workloads)
4. **Scorpio AI fabric switches** = direct play on scale-out networking, complements Broadcom Tomahawk on the merchant-silicon side

## Cross-reference

- [[NVIDIA]] reference designs include ALAB Aries for PCIe Gen 6 signal conditioning
- Hyperscaler ASIC programs (Microsoft Maia, Google TPU, AWS Trainium) all use ALAB connectivity solutions
- [[Broadcom]] competes more on switch ASICs; ALAB more on retimers + fabric switches at the rack-scale
