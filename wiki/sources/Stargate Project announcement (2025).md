| **Chairman** | Masayoshi Son (SoftBank) |

## Equity structure (ownership)

| Partner | Stake | Role | Capital commitment |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| **OpenAI** | 40% | Operational responsibility | $19B initial |
| **SoftBank** | 40% | Financial responsibility | $19B initial |
| **Oracle** | minority | Cloud/operations | ~$7B initial |
| **MGX (UAE sovereign)** | minority | Sovereign capital | ~$7B initial |

**Key technology partners (non-equity):** Arm, Microsoft, NVIDIA, Oracle, OpenAI

## Site rollout (cumulative as of October 2025)

US sites (5 new added Sept 2025 + Abilene flagship + CoreWeave projects = ~7 GW planned):
- **Abilene, TX** (flagship — JPMorgan $2.3B debt financing May 2025)
- Shackelford County, TX
- Milam County, TX
- Doña Ana County, NM
- Lordstown, OH
- One undisclosed Midwest location
- CoreWeave-developed projects

International:
- UAE (announced May 16, 2025)
- UK, Norway, Japan
- Argentina (Patagonia, $25B, 500 MW — Oct 10, 2025)

## Financing details

- SoftBank's first **$10B from Mizuho Bank**
- **JPMorgan Chase** lent **$2.3B for Abilene datacenters** (May 2025)
- Per Bloomberg (August 2025): "project had not started and no funds were raised meeting the $500B budget" — material caveat

## Reported stalling (Bloomberg, August 2025)

> "Stargate's $500 billion AI infrastructure project reportedly stalls over unresolved disputes between OpenAI, Oracle, and SoftBank"

Reasons cited:
- Market uncertainty
- American trade policy
- AI hardware valuations
- Unresolved partner disputes

This is a **material caveat** — the headline framing remains $500B/10GW but execution is at risk.
