## NVIDIA $2B investment — strategic significance

**NVIDIA committed $2B to Lumentum** for R&D + future capacity + new US fab.

This parallels [[Coherent]]'s NVIDIA equity stake:

| Vendor | NVIDIA backing |
| --- | --- |
| [[Coherent]] | NVIDIA equity stake (size undisclosed) |
| **Lumentum** | **$2B NVIDIA investment** |

Together, this is **explicit dual-sourcing** of the optical supply chain — NVIDIA cannot afford to be single-source dependent on either vendor for 1.6T/3.2T transceiver capacity.

## Why it matters for the bottleneck thesis

1. **Confirms optical transceiver dual-source strategy.** Coherent + Lumentum are now both NVIDIA-backed; the optical supply chain is strategically scaled up to match accelerator demand.
2. **+90% YoY revenue + 32% operating margins** = extreme operating leverage; comparable to [[SK Hynix]] HBM dynamics
3. **NVIDIA $2B capacity investment** = leading indicator for 1.6T/3.2T transceiver throughput multiplication into 2027-2028
4. **US fab construction** = supply chain resilience play; reduces optical concentration risk in Asia
