## Trainium economics commentary

Amazon explicitly stated Trainium expected to:
- **Save tens of billions in annual capex** vs relying on NVIDIA
- Provide **several hundred bps operating margin advantage** for inference
- Justifies the in-house silicon investment vs NVIDIA-only strategy

## Why it matters

1. **$225B Trainium commitments** is the cleanest evidence yet that hyperscaler custom silicon at scale is real, durable revenue (not just announcements)
2. **AWS +28% growth** is healthy but trails Azure (+40%) and Google Cloud (+63%) — market-share dynamics shifting
3. **$200B annual AI capex** = largest single hyperscaler capex committer; pressures FCF to $1.2B TTM = clearly approaching the limit of cash-funded capex sustainability
4. **>1M NVIDIA GPUs in 2026** matches AWS's commitment in [[NVIDIA Q1 FY27 earnings (2026)]] — primary cross-confirmation
5. **2.1M AI chips delivered** (>half Trainium) = AWS is rapidly diversifying from NVIDIA dependence
