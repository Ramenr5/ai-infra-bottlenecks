# Stargate Abilene

The **flagship site** of the [[Stargate]] project — first specific Texas location named in the Jan 2025 announcement. Part of the broader [[Stargate Texas]] umbrella (which now includes additional sites in Shackelford and Milam counties).

## Key facts

- **Location:** Abilene, Texas
- **Operator:** Stargate JV (OpenAI 40% / SoftBank 40% / Oracle ~$7B / MGX ~$7B)
- **Status:** Construction
- **Scale:** 10 datacenters at the Abilene location (per Stargate announcement)
- **Grid:** ERCOT
- **Anchor tenant:** [[OpenAI]]
- **First debt financing:** **JPMorgan Chase $2.3B loan** (May 2025) — specifically for the Abilene datacenters

## Why it matters

- **First named site** = first concrete proof point that Stargate is moving from announcement to construction
- **JPMorgan's $2.3B loan** is the canonical example of senior debt going into AI data center project finance at scale; useful comparable for future [[CoreWeave]], [[Crusoe Energy]], and similar non-recourse facility-level debt
- **Anchors the [[Stargate Texas]] portfolio** — Abilene is the operating reality vs. the announced 20-site / 10 GW headline framing
- **Test case for the broader Stargate execution thesis** — per Bloomberg (August 2025), Stargate as a whole has reportedly stalled on dispute resolution; Abilene's progress (or lack thereof) is the leading indicator
