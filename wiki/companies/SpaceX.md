## Why SpaceX matters for the AI infra thesis

1. **Starlink = backup connectivity for AI infrastructure** — remote/rural data centers, edge deployments, defense AI applications
2. **Sister relationship with xAI** — Elon Musk's other company built [[Colossus (xAI)]] in Memphis; shared engineering DNA and procurement leverage
3. **IPO event** = creates $T+ market cap entrant in space/connectivity; major capital re-allocation event in tech markets
4. **Starshield** = defense satellite division; adjacent to sovereign AI / national security AI thesis (significant DoD relationship)
5. **Capital absorption** — SpaceX itself is a major capital deployer; post-IPO it would have ~$80B fresh capital to deploy into Starlink constellation, Starship, and adjacent ventures (potentially including AI)

## Business segments

- **Launch services** — Falcon 9, Falcon Heavy, Starship — commercial + NASA + DoD launches
- **Starlink** — satellite internet (61% of total SpaceX revenue 2025)
- **Starshield** — defense / classified satellite (revenue not disclosed; growing fast)
- **Crew Dragon** — NASA + commercial crew transport
