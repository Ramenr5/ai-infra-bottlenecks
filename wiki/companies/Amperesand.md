# Amperesand

Solid State Transformer (SST) startup pursuing **both LV-input and MV-input** product lines. Targeting **30 MW of commercial deployments in 2026**.

## Key facts

- **Product strategy**: LV-input SST skids (3.2-4.8 MW) deployable today alongside AC distribution, with MV-input variants following as 3,300V-class SiC matures
- **Efficiency claim**: third-generation system >98.5%
- **2026 commercial deployment target**: 30 MW
- **Status**: pre-UL-certification as of May 2026

## Why it matters for the thesis

- **Dual-path strategy** hedges the SiC-supply timing risk: LV-first → revenue today; MV-input → endgame Phase 4 position
- 30 MW 2026 commercial target = early validation that SST adoption is real, not just pilots
- Pure-play exposure to [[800VDC transition]] across Phases 3-4

## Risks

- Pre-UL-cert; per-site AHJ approval needed
- LV-input variants are competitive against existing AC rectifiers + Phase 1-2 power racks → less differentiated near-term
- Competition with [[DG Matrix]] (NVIDIA MGX inclusion advantage) and [[Heron Power]] (manufacturing scale)
