# Dell Technologies

US server/infrastructure OEM (NYSE: DELL). A prime **AI-server demand proxy** — Dell's AI-optimized server backlog and order intake are among the cleanest box-maker tells on whether hyperscaler/enterprise AI demand is accelerating. A core input to the [[AI investment momentum tracker]].

## Why it matters for the thesis

- **Backlog = demand the supply chain can't yet ship.** Dell's AI-server backlog growing faster than shipments is a direct "demand > supply" signal, complementary to [[NVIDIA]] (chips), [[CoreWeave]] (cloud) and [[CoWoS packaging capacity]] (packaging)
- Dell integrates NVIDIA GPUs into rack-scale systems → its order book is a near-real-time read on downstream deployment pace
- Lower-margin than the silicon/cloud layers, but the **highest-frequency demand signal** (reports off-cycle from the calendar-quarter pack)
