# Crusoe Energy

**Private neocloud + datacenter developer** specializing in onsite-power AI infrastructure. Built the [[Stargate Abilene]] facility (operated under Oracle / OpenAI contract). Pioneer of "stranded gas → onsite generation → AI compute" model. Repeatedly mentioned across our wiki; previously a red wikilink, now a dedicated page.

## Key operational facts (per [[SemiAnalysis - Onsite Gas Deep Dive (2025)]])

- **Abilene facility (Stargate flagship)**: 360 MW nameplate generation
  - 5× **[[GE Vernova]] LM2500XPRESS aeroderivatives**
  - 5× **Solar Titan 350 IGTs** (Caterpillar subsidiary)
- **Boom Supersonic / Crusoe contract: 1.2 GW** turbine deployment
- Pioneer in using stranded natural gas at well-heads → on-site generation → mobile-or-fixed AI datacenters

## Business model

- **Neocloud architecture:** owns/operates compute infrastructure, leases capacity to AI labs + hyperscalers
- **Onsite gas-powered datacenters:** primary differentiation
- Customer base: OpenAI (via Stargate Abilene), Oracle, others
- Funding: private; backed by investors including G2 Venture Partners, Bain Capital Ventures, others
- US fab + facility footprint expanding

## Why it matters

1. **Crusoe operates [[Stargate Abilene]]** — the flagship Stargate site that JPMorgan financed $2.3B for in May 2025
2. **Stranded-gas onsite-power model** is a fundamentally different cost structure than grid-sourced — bypasses the 5-year grid interconnect queue (per [[SemiAnalysis - Onsite Gas Deep Dive (2025)]])
3. **One of a handful of neoclouds with hyperscaler-scale contracts** alongside [[CoreWeave]], Lambda, Nebius
4. Private; IPO speculation ongoing

## Counterparties

- [[OpenAI]] = anchor customer at Abilene (via [[Stargate]] / Oracle)
- [[Oracle]] = compute operator on top of Crusoe infrastructure
- [[GE Vernova]] = primary turbine supplier (LM2500XPRESS)
- Caterpillar = Solar Titan turbine supplier (CAT subsidiary)
- Boom Supersonic = 1.2 GW turbine vendor
