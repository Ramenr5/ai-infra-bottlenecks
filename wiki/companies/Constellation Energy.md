# Constellation Energy

Largest US nuclear operator (NASDAQ: CEG). The purest large-cap nuclear-IPP play on AI datacenter baseload demand — a nuclear-DC-pairing name in the [[Capex trajectory thesis]] tradable read.

## Why it matters for the thesis

- **Largest US nuclear fleet** = the most 24/7 carbon-free baseload to sell to hyperscalers seeking firm, clean power for AI datacenters
- **Co-location / behind-the-meter precedent**: nuclear-DC pairing (cf. [[Talen Energy]]'s Susquehanna–Amazon deal) is the template; Constellation has the fleet scale to do more
- **Crane Clean Energy Center** (Three Mile Island Unit 1 restart) — the landmark nuclear-restart-for-datacenter deal (Microsoft offtake) that signaled nuclear's AI-driven revival
