# Novos Power

US-based Solid State Transformer (SST) startup. Direct **medium-voltage to 800VDC** SST with **50% smaller footprint** vs competitors, using proprietary insulation and **air cooling** (most peers liquid-cooled).

## Key facts

- **Product**: direct MV-to-800VDC SST
- **Differentiation**: 50% smaller footprint; air cooling via proprietary insulation
- **Peak efficiency**: >98% reported
- **Status**: pre-UL-certification as of May 2026
- **Contributed to**: [[SemiAnalysis - Inside the 800VDC Revolution (2026)]] (acknowledged contributor)

## Why it matters for the thesis

- **Air cooling** is operationally simpler than competitors' liquid cooling — reduces deployment friction and BoM complexity for colocation operators
- Smaller footprint = white-space density advantage for retrofit deployments
- Direct MV-input = Phase 4 endgame target (vs LV-first incumbents)

## Risks

- Air cooling at high power density faces thermal headroom limits as SST capacity scales toward 6 MW
- Pre-UL-cert; every deployment requires per-site AHJ approval
- Smaller scale than [[Heron Power]] (40 GW disclosed manufacturing) or [[DG Matrix]] (NVIDIA MGX inclusion)
- Less public scaling commitment disclosed
