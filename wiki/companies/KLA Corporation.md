# KLA Corporation

**Process control oligopoly leader.** Holds **~58% market share in semiconductor inspection and metrology** — every wafer goes through KLA tools multiple times during fab processing. Less cyclical than [[Applied Materials]] or [[Lam Research]] because process control is mandatory regardless of process node or end-market.

## Why it matters for the bottleneck thesis

1. **Process control monopoly** — ~58% market share, no near peer (Onto Innovation, Applied Process Diagnostics distant followers). Every wafer must pass through inspection/metrology multiple times to identify defects and validate process windows. **Mandatory equipment regardless of node.**
2. **Advanced packaging $1B+ in FY26** = direct primary confirmation of [[CoWoS packaging capacity]] expansion. TSMC's CoWoS line buys KLA tools at every step.
3. **Less cyclical than AMAT/LRCX** — service revenue 23% + mandatory process control = baseline demand stable through cycles
4. **62/38 foundry-logic/memory split** mirrors industry capacity-add ratio across foundries (TSMC, Samsung, Intel) and memory makers (SK Hynix, Samsung Memory, Micron)
5. **High-margin equipment** — KLA gross margins historically in the 60% range, the highest among WFE vendors

## Strategic position
