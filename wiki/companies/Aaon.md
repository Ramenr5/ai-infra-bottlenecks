# Aaon

US HVAC / thermal-equipment manufacturer (NASDAQ: AAON). Historically commercial HVAC; **rapid datacenter-cooling growth via deep hyperscaler partnerships and customized solutions** per [[Dell'Oro - Liquid Cooling Market (2026)|Dell'Oro]].

## Key facts

- **Products**: custom air-handling, chillers, datacenter cooling systems; moving into liquid-cooling-adjacent thermal
- **Growth driver**: hyperscaler-customized cooling solutions (Dell'Oro singles out Aaon's "rapid growth")
- **Differentiator**: build-to-spec for hyperscalers vs off-the-shelf
- **End markets**: datacenter (fast-growing), commercial HVAC (legacy)

## Why it matters for the thesis

- Smaller-cap, higher-beta datacenter-cooling play; the "customized for hyperscalers" angle mirrors why AWS built its own CDU — Aaon captures the bespoke demand
- Cooling-transition exposure with a commercial-HVAC ballast

## Risks

- Hyperscaler concentration — customized solutions = customer-specific risk
- Commercial HVAC cyclicality in the legacy book
- Less liquid-cooling IP depth than [[CoolIT Systems]] / [[Vertiv Holdings]]; more air/thermal than pure DLC
