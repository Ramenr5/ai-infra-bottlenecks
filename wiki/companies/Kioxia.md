# Kioxia

**Japanese NAND flash supplier** (formerly Toshiba Memory). IPO'd late 2024 on Tokyo Stock Exchange. Plans US ADR listing. **Pure-play NAND** alongside [[SanDisk]] but Japanese-listed. Co-development partnership with Western Digital (now [[SanDisk]] post-spin) for shared R&D and manufacturing.

## Product / technology

- **BiCS FLASH** — Kioxia's 3D NAND architecture (industry-respected)
- **8th-gen and 10th-gen BiCS** in development; 8th-gen QLC in production
- **KIOXIA EG7 Series SSDs** — first client product on BiCS gen 8 QLC; TLC-equivalent performance + better TCO
- BiCS naming convention: "Bit Cost Scalable" — Kioxia's brand for 3D NAND generations

## Western Digital / SanDisk relationship

- Legacy Toshiba/SanDisk JV operates joint NAND fabs in Yokkaichi (Japan)
- Shared R&D + manufacturing scale = lower per-bit cost
- Post-2025 spin of SanDisk from Western Digital, partnership continues but ownership/cap structure shifted
- Combined Kioxia + SanDisk capacity = meaningful share of global NAND supply
