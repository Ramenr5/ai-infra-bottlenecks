# Stargate

A multi-year joint-venture vehicle for building large-scale AI datacenter and power infrastructure in the United States. Not a stand-alone operating company — more a project-finance structure with named anchor partners.

## Oracle $300B commitment to OpenAI — primary verified (May 2026)

Per [[Oracle Q3 FY26 earnings]]: Oracle has disclosed a **$300B agreement with OpenAI** for data center capacity across **4.5 GW** of planned facilities. This is a primary-source verification of one component of the broader $500B/10GW Stargate framework. Oracle is funding this via **$45-50B planned debt + equity raise** + $50B FY26 capex (up from $21.2B FY25). The Bloomberg stalling narrative from Aug 2025 thus appears at least partially resolved on the Oracle side — execution is proceeding even if the SoftBank portion remains uncertain.

## Structure

- **Demand anchor:** OpenAI as the primary compute consumer (offtake agreement governs project finance).
- **Operations:** Oracle providing cloud/operations layer.
- **Capital:** SoftBank and MGX (UAE sovereign) as primary equity contributors; debt structure not public.
- **Geographic concentration:** all initial sites in [[Texas]] (ERCOT grid jurisdiction, favorable permitting, abundant gas).

## Why it matters for the bottleneck thesis

- **Power-layer signal:** 10 GW for one project is roughly 2–3× the entire IT load of [[Northern Virginia]] (today's largest US DC cluster). Single biggest concrete data point in our wiki for how big the power-demand step-change is.
- **Capital-structure signal:** demonstrates that sovereign capital ([[MGX]]) is now part of US AI infrastructure financing, not just domestic hyperscaler balance sheets. See [[Capital flows & deal structures]].
- **Workforce/EPC signal:** 20 simultaneous 500K-sqft datacenters concentrated in Texas implies large absorption of EPC contractor capacity ([[Quanta Services]], [[Jacobs]], [[Fluor]]) and electrician labor.

## Open questions

- Construction sequencing: is the 10 GW build serial, parallel, or staged? Public communications suggest staged but exact cadence isn't published.
- Power sourcing: how much is on-site gas vs. grid via ERCOT vs. dedicated new nuclear/SMR?
- Debt vs. equity ratio in the cap stack; refinancing risk if SoftBank's balance sheet tightens.
- Will Stargate hit the 4-year cadence or slip 2+ years?
