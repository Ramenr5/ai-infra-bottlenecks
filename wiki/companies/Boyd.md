# Boyd

US engineered-materials and thermal-management company (private; Goldman Sachs-backed). Cold plates + liquid-cooling systems for AI datacenters. Named by [[Dell'Oro - Liquid Cooling Market (2026)|Dell'Oro]] among liquid-cooling share leaders.

## Key facts

- **Products**: cold plates, liquid-cooling loops, thermal interface materials, engineered thermal systems
- **Heritage**: broad thermal/sealing/EMI materials business; datacenter liquid cooling is the AI-growth vector
- **Ownership**: private (Goldman Sachs Capital Partners)
- **Market position**: strong liquid-cooling share (Dell'Oro)

## Why it matters for the thesis
