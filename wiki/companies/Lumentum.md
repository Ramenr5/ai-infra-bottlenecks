# Lumentum

**Optical transceiver leader** — one of the two major vendors (with [[Coherent]]) supplying 800G and 1.6T pluggables that backbone scale-out AI networking. **NVIDIA committed $2B investment** in Lumentum for R&D + future capacity + new US fab — explicit dual-sourcing strategy mirroring Coherent's NVIDIA equity backing.

## Product portfolio

- **1.6T DR4 OSFP pluggable transceivers** — debuted March 2026; 200G-per-lane EML technology; doubles 800G bandwidth
- 800G transceivers — current volume product
- ROADM (Reconfigurable Optical Add-Drop Multiplexer) systems for telecom
- Industrial lasers (legacy / non-AI segment)

## NVIDIA $2B investment (the strategic anchor)

NVIDIA committed **$2 billion** to Lumentum for:
- R&D acceleration
- Future production capacity
- New US-based manufacturing fab construction

This parallels NVIDIA's equity stake in [[Coherent]] — both are now strategically anchored optical suppliers. NVIDIA dual-sources to avoid single-vendor dependency at the optical layer.

## Why it matters for the bottleneck thesis

1. **+90% YoY revenue + 32% operating margin** = comparable economics to [[SK Hynix]] HBM dynamics. Optical transceiver scarcity is real and being monetized.
2. **NVIDIA $2B investment** = leading indicator of 1.6T/3.2T transceiver throughput expansion into 2027-2028; ensures the scale-out networking buildout can keep pace
3. **US fab construction** = supply chain resilience play; reduces optical concentration in Asia
4. **1.6T product cycle** = the bridge to hyperscaler 2027-2028 buildouts — Vera Rubin, AMD MI500-series all need 1.6T

## Counterparties / competitive frame

- [[Coherent]] — direct peer; both NVIDIA-backed
- Fabrinet — assembly partner / occasional competitor
- Optoelectronics IDM-side suppliers
- **Long-term threat: co-packaged optics (CPO)** — per [[SemiAnalysis - Co-Packaged Optics (2026)]]: scale-out adoption "limited" near-term (TCO benefits "not compelling"); scale-up CPO is **killer app for late 2020s** — Rubin Ultra NVL576 (late 2027) + Trainium 4 with Celestial AI CPO. Pluggable scale-out intact through 2027+; scale-up disruption from 2027-2028. Material long-term margin pressure.
