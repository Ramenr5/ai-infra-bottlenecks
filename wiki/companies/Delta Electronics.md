# Delta Electronics

Taiwanese power electronics and thermal management leader (TWSE: 2308). Aggressive 800VDC product launches across multiple categories: **110 kW BBU power shelves, 2.4 MW DC In-Row CDU (first major DC-native cooling product), 800VDC air-cooled busway**.

## Key facts

- **110 kW power shelves with 80 kW BBU each** = 480 kW total BBU capacity per 6-shelf rack (announced GTC 2026)
- **2.4 MW In-Row CDU supporting 800VDC** (GTC 2026) — first major DC-native cooling component announced
- **800VDC air-cooled busway** — demonstrated at OCP 2025
- **Compute tray power module**: on-blade 800V-to-50V step-down (Kyber rack architecture)
- **PSU module standard**: front-of-rack accessible, hot-swappable per Diablo 400 spec

## Why it matters for the thesis

- **Most product-rich 800VDC vendor** across PSU + BBU + busway + DC-native cooling — broadest exposure of any incumbent
- **Diablo 400 spec ecosystem leader** — standardized PSU shelves work with multi-vendor BBUs (Advanced Energy, multiple suppliers)
- DC-native CDU is a category-defining product (most auxiliary loads still on AC)
- Taiwan listing = direct exposure for non-US investors

## Risks

- TWSE listing access friction; ADR is DELHY (OTC, illiquid)
- Diversified revenue — automation, EV, building automation dilute AI datacenter exposure
- Competition with [[ABB]] (NVIDIA partnership), [[Eaton]] (Resilient acquisition), Infineon (component-level)
