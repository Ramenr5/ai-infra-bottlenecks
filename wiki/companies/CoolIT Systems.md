# CoolIT Systems

Canadian direct-liquid-cooling (DLC) pure-play (private; KKR-backed). Cold plates + CDUs for high-density AI racks. Named by [[Dell'Oro - Liquid Cooling Market (2026)|Dell'Oro]] among liquid-cooling share leaders.

## Key facts

- **Products**: cold plates, CDUs (rack + row), coolant distribution, DLC systems
- **Focus**: pure DLC (vs Vertiv/nVent's broader portfolios) — direct exposure to the 120 kW+/rack transition
- **Ownership**: private (KKR acquired a majority stake in 2023)
- **Market position**: strong liquid-cooling share (Dell'Oro)

## Why it matters for the thesis

- One of the purest DLC plays, but **private** — relevant for: (a) M&A/IPO watch, (b) understanding the competitive set for public names [[Vertiv Holdings]] / [[nVent Electric]]
- KKR ownership = private-equity validation of the cooling-capex thesis
- Cold-plate + CDU focus = direct beneficiary of GB200/Rubin DLC mandate

## Risks

- Private — no direct equity access (IPO or trade-sale optionality only)
- Pure DLC = no ballast if transition timing slips
- Hyperscaler insourcing (AWS proprietary CDU) threatens merchant DLC vendors most
