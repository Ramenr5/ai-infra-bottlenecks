# OpenAI

US frontier AI lab; the single largest compute-demand anchor in the wiki (17+ inbound references). Co-anchor of [[Stargate]] (the $500B / 10 GW buildout) and the demand-side driver behind much of the [[Capex trajectory thesis]]. Consumer-skewed revenue (ChatGPT) vs [[Anthropic]]'s API/enterprise skew.

## Why it's central to the thesis

- **[[Stargate]] anchor**: OpenAI is the lead tenant of the $500B / 10 GW Stargate program (40% OpenAI / 40% SoftBank / Oracle + MGX), flagship site [[Stargate Abilene]] (per [[Stargate Project announcement (2025)]])
- **Multi-DC training**: racing to build distributed training clusters to catch Google's multi-datacenter lead (per [[SemiAnalysis - Multi-Datacenter Training]])
- **Compute commitments**: GPT-5.5 codesigned/trained/served on NVIDIA Blackwell; **$300B Oracle deal across 4.5 GW** (per [[Oracle Q3 FY26 earnings]]); $24.6B / 750 MW Cerebras inference MRA (per [[Cerebras — Faster Tokens Please (2026)]])
- **TaaS distribution**: recently added to AWS Bedrock alongside Anthropic (per [[SemiAnalysis - Anthropic Bedrock AWS Margins (2026)]])
