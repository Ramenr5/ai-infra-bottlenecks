# Eaton

Diversified power management incumbent (NYSE: ETN). Major datacenter electrical equipment supplier; **acquired Resilient Power Systems August 2025** to add SST expertise to its 800VDC roadmap.

## Key facts

- **Acquisition**: Resilient Power Systems acquired August 2025 — explicit SST expertise play
- **Product range**: switchboards, UPS, busway, switchgear, ATS (full AC datacenter stack)
- **800VDC positioning**: incumbent with no named discrete 800VDC switchboard product as of May 2026; positioning is via M&A rather than internal product launches
- **Customer set**: datacenter operators (hyperscale + colo), industrial, utility, commercial buildings

## Why it matters for the thesis

- **Resilient acquisition** is the cleanest signal that an incumbent power-management vendor sees SST as material to its datacenter roadmap
- Diversified exposure: AC distribution today + SST optionality → less binary than [[DG Matrix]] / [[Heron Power]] / [[Amperesand]] / [[Novos Power]] pure-plays
- Existing customer relationships across hyperscalers give Eaton distribution power that pure-play SST startups must build from scratch

## Risks

- Diversified revenue means AI datacenter exposure is diluted within other segments (utility, commercial, vehicle)
- Resilient is a relatively quiet startup; integration risk vs. organic product investment
- AC-distribution legacy revenue at risk of cannibalization as 800VDC takes share through 2030
