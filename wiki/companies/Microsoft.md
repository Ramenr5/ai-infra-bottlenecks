## Custom silicon

- **Maia 100** — AI accelerator in production
- **Cobalt 100** — Arm-based CPU (via [[ARM Holdings]] CSS) in production
- **50% better price-performance** vs off-the-shelf for internal workloads
- Maia 200 + Cobalt deployed across nearly half of data center regions
- Designed with [[Broadcom]] / [[Marvell Technology]] (custom ASIC partners)

## Strategic position

- **OpenAI anchor relationship** restructured in 2026; specifics limited
- Microsoft + [[Stargate]] = both Microsoft-funded compute + JV-funded compute serving [[OpenAI]]
- Azure competes head-to-head with AWS, Google Cloud — currently +40% growth puts it between AWS (+28%) and Google Cloud (+63%)
- Capital deployment > FCF — Microsoft tapping debt markets for AI capex
