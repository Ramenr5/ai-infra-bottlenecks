## Business segments

- **Power** — gas turbines, steam turbines, hydro
- **Electrification** — grid equipment, transformers, switchgear, automation
- **Wind** — onshore + offshore wind turbines
- Gas turbines = the high-margin, high-demand segment driven by AI

## Why it matters

1. **Gas turbine supercycle confirmed.** 110 GW backlog target for YE 2026 = ~2× the [[Goldman Sachs - Generational Growth (2024)|Goldman 47 GW US incremental capacity]] number from GE Vernova alone (global demand vs US-only Goldman).
2. **$2.4B data center equipment orders in Q1** > all of 2025 = order pace acceleration
3. **$200B backlog target pulled forward by a year** = leading indicator on duration of build-out
4. **Owns the gas turbine** powering behind-the-meter generation for [[Stargate]], merchant gas builds, [[Susquehanna nuclear plant|nuclear-paired DCs]]
5. Direct beneficiary of [[Goldman Sachs - Generational Growth (2024)]] 60% gas mix in new US generation
