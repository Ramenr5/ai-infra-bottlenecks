# SemiAnalysis

Independent semiconductor and AI infrastructure analyst shop founded by **Dylan Patel**. Probably the most influential public-facing analyst on AI hardware supply chains — peer of [[Epoch AI]] in methodological rigor, but with broader vendor/industry coverage.

## Why it's tracked here as a "company"

Like [[Epoch AI]], SemiAnalysis functions as a **primary analyst source** in our wiki. We expect to re-ingest their work repeatedly across multiple bottleneck domains (HBM, CoWoS, hyperscaler capex, neoclouds, inference pricing).

## Key people

- **Dylan Patel** — founder, chief analyst; primary voice on AI infrastructure
- **Myron Xie** — author of the Cerebras IPO deep-dive (and the source page that landed here)
- Substantial research team (several authors across newsletter, Tokenomics Model, InferenceX)

## Notable products

- **Newsletter** — paywalled deep-dives on specific companies and topics (the Cerebras source is a public/free portion of one such piece)
- **AI Datacenter Industry Model** — capacity tracker (cited by Epoch and others)
- **Accelerator Model** — chip shipment / revenue forecasts (cited in the Cerebras piece for Cerebras's revenue inflection)
- **Tokenomics Model** — OpenAI / Anthropic revenue broken down by model + pricing tier
- **InferenceX** — open-source inference benchmark across hardware vendors; builds the full throughput/interactivity Pareto frontier
- **AgentX dashboard** — anonymized production traces from Claude Code, Codex, Cursor, OpenCode (432K requests / ~80B tokens sample)
- **tokenomics.info** — interactive calculators (e.g. Cerebras WSE BOM analysis)

## Why it matters for us

- **Primary methodological anchor** for the compute/datacenter layer, paralleling [[Epoch AI]]'s position on scaling forecasts
- **Bias toward operator/engineer perspective.** Specs, BOMs, kernel-level analysis, real production traces — distinct from analyst reports that work top-down from financial models
- **Forward-citing impact.** Their model outputs (capacity, GPU shipments, cooling specs) get cited by hyperscalers, government reports, and academic forecasts
- **Note on Tokenomics framing:** the "fast inference is a paid product" thesis ([[Cerebras — Faster Tokens Please (2026)]]) was largely surfaced by SemiAnalysis's interactivity-tier analysis — a market dynamic we'd otherwise have missed in throughput-only framings

## What to watch

- Periodic NVIDIA, AMD, hyperscaler-ASIC deep-dives
- Power/grid-side analysis (less their historical core but expanding)
- Their take on the [[AI debt financing capacity]] dynamic as it develops
- Public InferenceX dashboard releases (currently selective)
