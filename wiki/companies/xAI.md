# xAI

US frontier AI lab (Elon Musk). Builder of the [[Colossus (xAI)]] supercomputers — notable for **record-fast datacenter construction** and aggressive onsite-gas power deployment. A meaningful compute-demand anchor and sister entity to [[SpaceX]].

## Why it matters for the thesis

- **[[Colossus (xAI)]]**: 100K GPUs built in 122 days (Colossus 1, Memphis); **Colossus 2 = first GW-scale datacenter**, 1.1 GW target (1.5 GW expansion), 3.7× Colossus 1 (per [[SemiAnalysis - xAI Colossus 2 (2026)]])
- **Onsite gas**: xAI Memphis Colossus 2 = **1.9 GW Doosan Enerbility** turbines behind-the-meter (per [[SemiAnalysis - Onsite Gas Deep Dive (2025)]]) — the canonical "bypass the grid queue" deployment
- **Construction speed** as a competitive weapon: cross-state regulatory arbitrage, refurbished-factory reuse
- **Capital**: $40B Saudi PIF raise rumored (per [[SemiAnalysis - xAI Colossus 2 (2026)]]); sister relationship with [[SpaceX]]
