# CoreWeave

Largest pure-play AI neocloud (NASDAQ: CRWV). Rents NVIDIA GPU capacity at scale, funded heavily via **non-recourse debt secured against hyperscaler-contracted offtake** — making it the archetype (and stress-test case) for the [[AI debt financing capacity]] bottleneck.

## Why it matters for the thesis

- **The debt-financing canary**: CoreWeave's model (build GPU clusters on debt against contracted offtake) is the mechanism the [[AI debt financing capacity]] page watches — spread widening directly raises its build cost
- **Margin-disadvantaged vs hyperscalers**: per [[SemiAnalysis - How Much Do GPU Clusters Really Cost (2026)]], neoclouds compete on price but lack the TaaS margin engine hyperscalers have (AWS Bedrock etc.) — CoreWeave is wholesale IaaS, the lowest-margin tier
- **Bear-case exposure**: flagged in [[Capex bust scenario]] as a most-exposed name if capex rationalizes (high-beta, debt-dependent, single-bottleneck)
