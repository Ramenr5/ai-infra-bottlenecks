# KLA Q3 FY26 — extracted

## Financials

- **Q3 FY26 revenue: $3.415B** (+11% YoY, +4% sequential — above midpoint)
- **Non-GAAP EPS: $9.40** (above midpoint)
- $7B buyback program announced
- **Inspection/metrology market share: 58%** (Q3 FY26 disclosure)

## Semiconductor Process Control segment

- **$3.08B** (90.3% of total revenue) — +12.6% YoY, +3% sequential
- End-market mix: **62% foundry/logic, 38% memory** (systems basis)
- **Wafer inspection: $1.74B (51% of revenue)** — +16% YoY, +11% sequential

## Service revenue (recurring)

- **$775M (22.7% of total)** — +15.8% YoY
- Recurring high-margin business

## Advanced packaging breakout (NEW)

- **Advanced Wafer-Level Packaging:** +14 percentage points of market share gained
- **~70% YoY revenue growth in advanced packaging**
- ~$1B advanced packaging business **this year** (up from ~$635M in 2025)
- Direct exposure to [[CoWoS packaging capacity]] expansion

## Strategic position

- WFE oligopoly — **dominant in inspection/metrology** (~58% market share)
- Every wafer goes through KLA tools multiple times in fab
- Less direct cyclical exposure than AMAT/LRCX — process control is mandatory regardless of node
