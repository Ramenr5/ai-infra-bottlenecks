# Lumentum Q3 FY26 — extracted

## Financials

- **Q3 FY26 revenue: $808.4M** (+90.1% YoY — RECORD)
- **GAAP net income: $144.2M ($1.50/share GAAP diluted)** — vs $44.1M loss YA
- **GAAP GM: 44.2%; Non-GAAP GM: 47.9%**
- **Non-GAAP operating margin: 32.2%** (vs 10.8% YA)

## Product / strategic

- **1.6T DR4 OSFP pluggable transceivers debuted March 2026**, using 200G-per-lane EML technology — doubles 800G bandwidth
- High-speed 800G + 1.6T mix shift drove margin expansion
- Positioned for the "scale-up bandwidth wall" — GPU-to-GPU communication speeds

## NVIDIA $2B investment

- **NVIDIA investing $2 BILLION in Lumentum** for R&D + future capacity + new US fab
- Strategic vendor partnership comparable to [[Coherent]]'s NVIDIA equity backing — two anchors for the optical supply chain

## Strategic position

- Direct peer to [[Coherent]] in optical transceivers
- NVIDIA dual-sourcing across both via equity backing/investment
- 1.6T product cycle = bridge to 2027-2028 hyperscaler buildouts
