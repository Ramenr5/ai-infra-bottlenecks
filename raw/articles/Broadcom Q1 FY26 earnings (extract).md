# Broadcom Q1 FY26 — extracted

## AI revenue

- **Q1 AI revenue: $8.4B (+106% YoY)** — driven by custom AI accelerators + AI networking
- **Q2 FY26 AI revenue guide: $10.7B (+140% YoY)** — accelerating
- CEO Hock Tan: "**line of sight to achieve AI revenue from chips in excess of $100B in 2027**"
- **$73B backlog** of committed customer orders for AI

## Custom AI ASICs (XPUs)

- Customers: Google (TPU), Meta (MTIA), OpenAI, Anthropic
- Broadcom is the design partner / fabrication coordinator (works with TSMC for the actual silicon)
- Multi-billion-dollar relationships per customer

## Networking products

- **Tomahawk 6 switch chip:** entered volume production March 2026 — **industry's first 102.4 Tbps Ethernet switch ASIC**
- **Jericho 4 fabric chip (51.2 Tbps):** began shipping Aug 2025
- Designed to interconnect **>1 million XPUs across data centers**
- Dominant in Ethernet-based data center fabric

## Strategic context

- Broadcom is the **other major custom AI ASIC partner** alongside Marvell (more concentrated on Google + Meta)
- Networking ASICs are nearly monopoly position for hyperscaler Ethernet switches

Source: Broadcom 8-K + multiple syntheses (Finovian, Tom's Hardware, longyield Substack).
