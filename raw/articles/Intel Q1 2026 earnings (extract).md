# Intel Q1 2026 — extracted

## Financials

- **Q1 2026 revenue: $13.58B** (+7.2% YoY — beat $12.42B consensus)
- **AI-driven businesses now 60% of Intel's total revenue**, +40% YoY
- Sixth straight beat — "New Intel" framing

## DCAI (Data Center + AI)

- **DCAI revenue: $5.1B** (+22% YoY, +7% sequential)
- **AI-specific revenue within DCAI: >$750M Q1** (vs ~$400M YA)
- Growth: rising ASPs on Xeon 6 "Granite Rapids" + Gaudi 3 uptake in AWS + IBM Cloud
- **Gaudi 4 captured ~6% data center accelerator market share** — value-per-dollar play

## Panther Lake / 18A

- **Panther Lake (18A) volume ramping 6-7× QoQ**
- Q2 gross margin steps down from 41% → 39% on Panther Lake ramp
- **18A yields targeting year-end target hitting mid-year** (per CFO Zinsner)
- Panther Lake = Intel internal first product on 18A; launched Jan 2026

## Intel Foundry

- **Intel Foundry revenue: $5.4B** (+16% YoY)
- Operating loss narrowed: $2.51B Q4 2025 → **$1.8B Q1 2026**
- First foundry customer products on 18A starting to ramp

## Strategic position

- IDM (Integrated Device Manufacturer) — designs + manufactures own chips, plus foundry for others
- 18A = first Intel node to lead TSMC (vs trailing for ~5 years before)
- Foundry is structurally important — only US-headquartered leading-edge foundry alternative to TSMC
- AI accelerator (Gaudi 4) is a #3 player; NVIDIA + AMD dominant
