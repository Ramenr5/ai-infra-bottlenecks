# Tokyo Electron FY26 — extracted

## Q4 FY26 (Jan-Mar 2026)

- **Q4 net sales: 711.8B yen** (+28.9% sequential, +8.6% YoY)
- Shares up 6.89% on report; approached 52-week high

## Full FY26 (year ended March 2026) — RECORD

- **Net sales: 2.44 trillion yen** (+0.49% YoY)
- **Net income: 574.45B yen** (+5.57%)
- Record net sales AND record net income
- Driven by AI server + advanced logic demand

## Market share (Gartner / TechInsights data)

- **Dry etch systems: 23% share**
- **Deposition systems: 27% share**
- **Wafer probers: 38% share** (dominant)

## WFE outlook

- WFE market expected to grow **20%+ in calendar 2026 and 2027**
- WFE market reaching **$150-170B/year**
- **Etch systems specifically: +25%+ growth** expected
- Drivers: high-aspect-ratio contact, interconnect, gate-all-around processes

## Strategic position

- WFE oligopoly member (alongside ASML, AMAT, LRCX, KLA)
- Japanese conglomerate — different geographic exposure than US peers
- Dominant in wafer probers (38%); meaningful in etch/deposition (#3-4)
- Less directly tied to memory cycle than Lam Research; more diversified
