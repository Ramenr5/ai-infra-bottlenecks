# SanDisk Q3 FY26 — extracted

## Financials

- **Q3 FY26 revenue: ~$4.65B** (consensus expectation)
- **EPS: $14.30** (consensus expectation)
- Driven by AI-fueled enterprise storage demand + NAND price increases

## AI / data center commentary

- **AI servers account for >45% of shipments**
- **Data center revenue: +64% QoQ to $440M**
- Hyperscaler cloud spending is the structural demand driver

## NAND market dynamics

- **AI infrastructure buildout has created a structural NAND flash shortage expected to persist until 2028**
- This is a new bottleneck not previously surfaced in our wiki

## Stock performance + sell-side views

- **SanDisk spun from Western Digital February 2025**
- **Shares: <$50 → $697.99 (April 1, 2026) = +1,350% in 12 months**
- BofA (Wamsi Mohan): price target raised to **$1,080** from $900; Buy
- Evercore ISI (Amit Daryanani): initiated **Outperform, $1,200 PT**
- Thesis: "secular opportunity as AI inference makes NAND more indispensable"; "more durable NAND cycle supported by AI-led demand and tighter supply"

## Strategic position

- Pure-play NAND flash memory company (vs Micron's DRAM/HBM + NAND mix)
- Counterparts in NAND: Samsung (NAND), SK Hynix (NAND), Micron (NAND), Kioxia, YMTC
- Different bottleneck dimension than HBM — NAND is for storage/inference/KV cache
