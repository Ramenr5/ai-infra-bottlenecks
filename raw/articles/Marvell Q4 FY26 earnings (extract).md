# Marvell Q4 FY26 — extracted

## Financials

- **Q4 FY26 revenue: $2.219B** (+22% YoY)
- **Data center segment Q4: $1.65B** (quarterly RECORD)
- **FY26 total data center revenue: >$6B**
- **Q1 FY27 revenue guide: ~$2.4B (±5%)** — significantly above expectations

## Custom AI ASIC business

- Custom chip revenue went from near zero in FY26 to **annual run rate $1.5B**
- Expected to continue growing **>20% YoY in FY27** (faster than previously guided)
- Driven by hyperscaler infrastructure investments
- Microsoft Maia is publicly known customer

## Optical / interconnect

- Management guided interconnect/optical business at **50%+ YoY growth in FY27** (vs 20%+ for custom ASIC)
- Driven by 800G + 1.6T DSP penetration, CXL switch attach rates
- "Photonic Fabric" ramps eventually

## Strategic position

- "Engineering the backbone of the AI data center" — connectivity-heavy with custom ASIC option
- Adjacency to Astera Labs (connectivity) and Broadcom (custom ASIC + networking)

Source: Marvell IR + multiple analyst syntheses (BigGo, Tom's Hardware, AInvest).
