# Kioxia — extracted

## Background

- **Japanese NAND flash supplier**, formerly Toshiba Memory
- **IPO'd late 2024** on Tokyo Stock Exchange (ticker 285A)
- **Plans to list ADRs on US exchange** (timing not disclosed)
- Western Digital co-development partnership (shared R&D + manufacturing scale)

## Q1 FY26 earnings (April-June 2025, the trough quarter)

- EPS: ¥33.90
- **Revenue: ¥342.8B (-20% YoY)** — pre-AI-NAND-shortage tail of prior down-cycle
- Net income: ¥18.3B (-74% YoY)

## Q3 FY26 indicators (Oct-Dec 2025, the inflection)

- Simply Wall St cites **EPS surged to ¥162 in Q3 FY26** — material inflection vs the ¥34 trough
- Aligned with NAND price surge thesis ([[NAND flash supply]])

## Strategic / product

- **BiCS FLASH technology** — Kioxia's 3D NAND architecture
- **8th-gen and 10th-gen BiCS** in development; 8th-gen QLC in production
- **KIOXIA EG7 Series SSDs** — first client product on BiCS gen 8 QLC; TLC-equivalent performance with better TCO

## Strategic position

- One of the 5-6 major NAND suppliers globally
- Roughly tied with Western Digital / SanDisk legacy on volumes
- Less HBM/DRAM exposure than Micron / SK Hynix / Samsung
- Pure-play NAND like SanDisk but Japanese-listed

## Why timing matters

- Q1 FY26 (Jun 2025) was the trough quarter before NAND prices surged
- By Q3 FY26 (Dec 2025) and Q4 FY26 (Mar 2026) earnings will likely have inflected dramatically
- US ADR listing would broaden US investor access
