# Coherent Q3 FY26 — extracted

## Financials

- **Q3 FY26 revenue: $1.80B** (vs $1.41B Q3 FY25 — record)
- **Gross margin: 39.6%** (vs 38.5% Q3 FY25)
- NVIDIA-backed (NVIDIA holds equity stake)

## Data center / optical transceiver segment

- **Data Center revenue: +13% QoQ, +37% YoY**
- Growth drivers: **transceivers + optical circuit switches (OCS)**
- **800G and 1.6T transceivers** are key revenue drivers
- 1.6T ramping faster than expected, carries better early-lifecycle margins
- 1.6T will be growth driver through calendar 2026 and into 2027

## Indium Phosphide capacity (the upstream supply story)

- **6-inch indium phosphide produces >4× the devices at <half the cost** vs 3-inch
- First transceivers containing 6-inch components shipped this quarter
- **Capacity expected to double by end of calendar 2026** (one quarter ahead of plan)

## Strategic position

- One of the two major optical transceiver vendors (with [[Lumentum]])
- Direct exposure to NVIDIA NVL72 / NVL144 scale-out networking
- NVIDIA equity backing is meaningful — signals strategic vendor lock-in
