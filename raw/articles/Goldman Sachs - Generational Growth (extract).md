# Generational Growth — Goldman Sachs Research

**Verbatim figures from the report:**

> "2.4% CAGR in US power demand growth through 2030 from 2022 levels vs. ~0% over the last decade"

> "data centers will be 8% of total US power demand by 2030 from about 3% currently"

> "15% CAGR in data center power demand from 2023-2030"

> "$50 bn of capital investment in US power generation capacity cumulatively through 2030"

> "47 GW of incremental power generation capacity will be required to support US data center power demand growth cumulatively through 2030"

> "met with about 60% gas and 40% renewable sources"

> "data center power demand is poised to grow 160% by the end of the decade"
