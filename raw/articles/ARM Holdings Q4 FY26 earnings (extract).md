# ARM Q4 FY26 — extracted

- **Q4 revenue: $1.49B** (record quarterly; above midpoint of guide)
- **Royalty revenue: $671M** (+11% YoY)
- Royalty growth across smartphones, Edge AI, Physical AI, Cloud AI
- **Data center royalty more than doubled YoY**
- **Neoverse royalties doubled YoY**, expected to double again in FY27
- **CSS (Compute Subsystem) program adoption with AWS, Google, NVIDIA, Microsoft**
- **FY26 royalty revenue: $2.61B (+21%)**; FY26 licensing revenue: $2.31B (+25%)

Source: SEC 6-K filing FYE26 Q4 (exhibit992fye26q431-marx26.htm), plus Investing.com transcript coverage.
