# Power Integrations 1250V/1700V GaN for 800VDC AI Datacenters — Press Release Extract

## Date: October 13, 2025

## Product disclosures
- **1250V PowiGaN HEMTs**: industry-first; field-proven reliability; >98% efficiency targeting
- **1700V PowiGaN switch**: integrated in InnoMux2-EP IC; supports 1000VDC input; >90.3% 12V-system efficiency in SR ZVS operation
- **Cumulative shipped**: 175 million GaN switches (lifetime, all PowiGaN families)

## NVIDIA collaboration

> "Power Integrations is collaborating with NVIDIA to accelerate the transition to 800 VDC power."

## Executive quote

> "With rising AI power demands, moving to an 800 VDC input simplifies rack design, makes more efficient use of space and reduces copper usage."
> — **Roland Saint-Pierre, VP Product Development, Power Integrations**

## What is NOT in the release
- No specific design wins beyond NVIDIA collaboration
- No hyperscaler or server OEM named
- No sampling / volume / production timeline
- No mention of Diablo 400, Kyber rack, on-blade DC-DC architecture, or specific rack power levels (just "megawatt-scale racks")
- No competitive comparisons (vs Infineon, Wolfspeed, Navitas)

First public disclosure of POWI's NVIDIA collaboration on 800VDC. Q1 2026 earnings call (May 9, 2026) confirmed the collaboration is ongoing and includes "a variety of sockets."
