# Siemens Energy Q2 FY26 — extracted

- **Q2 FY26 orders: €17.7B (RECORD; +29.5% comparable)**
- **Q2 revenue: €10.3B** (+8.9%)
- Profit before special items: €1.16B (vs €906M YA)
- **Backlog: €154B (RECORD; book-to-bill 1.72)**
- Free cash flow pre-tax: €1.98B (vs €1.39B YA)
- **FY26 free cash flow pre-tax guide RAISED to ~€8B** (from prior €4-5B range)

## Gas Services segment (the data-center driver)

- **Orders +32.4% to €8.87B** — largest business contributor
- Revenue +15% to €3.48B
- **"U.S. demand largely driven by data centers"** — explicit AI data center attribution
- **US orders nearly doubled to €6.94B**; US revenue +45.7% to €2.75B
