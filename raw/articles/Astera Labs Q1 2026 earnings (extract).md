# Astera Labs Q1 2026 — extracted

## Financials

- **Q1 revenue: $308.4M** (+93% YoY, +14% QoQ — RECORD)
- **Non-GAAP gross margin: 76.4%** (mix shift toward AI fabric switches)
- Q2 guide: **$355-365M** (15-18% sequential growth); GM expected ~73% (non-cash warrant impact)

## Product lines

- **Scorpio AI Fabric Switches:** initial volume shipments of new 320-lane X-Series; on track to be largest product line by year-end; full production H2 2026
- **PCIe Gen 6 (Aries + Scorpio combined): >1/3 of total revenue**
- **Aries (PCIe/CXL retimers/smart cables):** early adoption across scale-up and scale-out platforms
- **Taurus (Ethernet smart cables / AECs):** broad demand from GPU/XPU/general-purpose systems
- **Leo (CXL memory controller):** early ramp with Microsoft Azure M-Series; secured custom KV Cache design shipping 2027

## Strategic context

- Pure-play AI connectivity silicon; merchant supplier alongside [[NVIDIA]] reference designs + hyperscaler ASIC programs
- Connectivity is the bottleneck between accelerator scaling and rack-scale deployment
