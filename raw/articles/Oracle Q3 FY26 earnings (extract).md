# Oracle Q3 FY26 — extracted

- **Q3 FY26 revenue: $17.2B** (+22% USD)
- **Cloud revenue: $8.9B (+44%)**
- **Remaining Performance Obligations (RPO): $553B (+325% YoY)** — record, driven by AI infra contracts
- **$300B agreement with OpenAI** to provide data center capacity across **4.5 GW** of planned facilities — primary RPO driver
- Part of $500B [[Stargate]] initiative (Oracle + SoftBank + NVIDIA + OpenAI)
- **FY26 capex: ~$50B** (up from $21.2B FY25)
- Planning **$45-50B debt + equity raise** to fund expansion
- Strategic positioning: "GPU landlord" for AI infrastructure, particularly serving OpenAI
- Layoffs of 30,000 reported alongside AI pivot — operational restructuring

Source: Oracle IR + IntuitionLabs + ERP Today + Phemex.
