# Stargate Project — extracted

## Initial announcement (January 21, 2025)

- **Total committed capital: $500 billion** to be deployed over 4 years
- **Initial deployment: $100 billion** immediately
- **Joint venture structure** announced by President Trump at the White House
- **Equity ownership:** OpenAI 40%, SoftBank 40%, with Oracle and MGX as additional equity holders
- **Capital contributions:** OpenAI $19B, SoftBank $19B, Oracle ~$7B, MGX ~$7B (initial commitments)
- **Roles:** SoftBank has financial responsibility, OpenAI has operational responsibility
- **Chairman:** Masayoshi Son (SoftBank)
- **Structure:** Delaware-incorporated joint venture
- **Key technology partners:** Arm, Microsoft, Nvidia, Oracle, OpenAI

## Capacity targets

- **10 gigawatts total** by end of 2025 (the original full commitment)
- **Currently planned: ~7 GW** (per Sept 2025 expansion announcement)
- Flagship site: **Abilene, Texas** — 10 datacenters

## Sites announced (as of Sept 2025)

US sites (5 new + Abilene flagship + CoreWeave projects = ~7 GW):
- Abilene, TX (flagship)
- Shackelford County, TX
- Milam County, TX
- Doña Ana County, NM
- Lordstown, OH
- One undisclosed Midwest location
- Plus CoreWeave-developed projects

International expansion:
- UAE (announced May 16, 2025)
- UK, Norway, Japan
- Argentina (Patagonia, $25B, 500MW — announced Oct 10, 2025)

## Financing details

- SoftBank's first $10B expected from **Mizuho Bank**
- **JPMorgan Chase lent $2.3B for Abilene datacenters** (May 2025)
- Per Bloomberg, August 2025: "project had not started and no funds were raised meeting the $500B budget"

## Reported challenges / stalling (Bloomberg August 2025)

- Project delays due to "market uncertainty, American trade policy, and AI hardware valuations"
- "Stalls over unresolved disputes between OpenAI, Oracle, and SoftBank" (per The Decoder coverage)
- This material caveat is widely reported but not resolved in our sources

## Significance / context

- Largest single AI infrastructure project announced to date
- Confirms the $500B / 10 GW headline framing (resolves the contradiction with the earlier Epoch source's $100B / 5 GW snapshot from 2024 — that was the original Microsoft/OpenAI campus framing, before the formal Stargate JV was structured)
- Introduces the **vendor-equity vertical integration** model with sovereign capital (MGX)
- Anchors [[Texas]] as the dominant US AI infra geography
- Single demand-side data point that drives ~10% of the McKinsey 156 GW 2030 AI capacity forecast
